TaskHuman
Aligning a fast-scaling wellness platform with enterprise ambition.

Start a Project

TaskHuman had grown rapidly but their brand had not kept pace. The goal was to mature the visual identity and redesign the website to reflect their enterprise positioning without losing the warmth that made the product human.

Role Brand & Digital Designer
Scope
Brand strategy Visual identity Website design Art direction
Year 2025
Outcome

The redesign marked the beginning of a multi year partnership with TaskHuman, supporting the evolution of both its brand and digital ecosystem. Across multiple phases, I led the refinement of its visual identity, developed a scalable design system, and directed the redesign of its website to better articulate the platform's value and maturity.

This sustained engagement helped reposition TaskHuman as a credible, category defining digital coaching platform, strengthening its market presence and supporting the momentum that led to the successful close of its $20 million Series B investment.
