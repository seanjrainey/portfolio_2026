Start a Project

Launch

For companies building something from the ground up.

You're starting a new venture, launching a development, or entering the market for the first time. You need a brand and digital presence that earns credibility from day one — not something you'll outgrow in six months.

I'll work with you to understand your positioning, your audience, and your goals — then build the foundation that gets you taken seriously before you ever walk into the room.

This typically includes a combination of: Brand strategy & identity, logo & visual system, website design & Webflow development, brand guidelines, and launch collateral.

Evolve

For companies that have outgrown what they started with.

Your business has changed. Maybe the logo was a favour from a friend. Maybe the website worked three years ago but now loses you deals. You don't need to start from scratch — you need someone who can see what's worth keeping and what needs to change.

I'll identify where perception and performance are misaligned — and rebuild the parts that are costing you credibility.

This typically includes a combination of: Brand refresh or redesign, website redesign & Webflow development, design system & guidelines, SEO fundamentals, and content strategy support.

Partner

For companies that need a designer who already knows their brand.

You don't have a one-off project. You have an ongoing gap — no senior design thinking in-house, no one who understands your brand deeply enough to move fast without a brief every time.

I work closely with a handful of companies at any one time, providing creative direction, design execution, and brand stewardship month to month. Think of it as a fractional design director — without the overhead.

Most partnerships begin after an initial Launch or Evolve engagement — once there's alignment and momentum.

How it works

1

We start with a conversation

Every engagement begins with a free 30-minute call. I'll learn about your business, your goals, and what's not working. No pitch, no pressure — just an honest conversation about whether I can help.

2

I send a clear proposal

If we're a good fit, I'll send a proposal with a defined scope, timeline, and fixed price. No hourly tracking, no surprise invoices — you know exactly what you're getting and what it costs.

I keep you close throughout the process — not bombarding you with updates, but never disappearing either. You'll know where we are, what's coming next, and why decisions were made.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this cost?

Every engagement is different, so I price based on scope and the impact of the work, not hours. After our initial conversation, I will send a proposal with clear options. Most brand and web projects fall between $8K and $40K depending on complexity. Ongoing partnerships are priced monthly.

Do you charge hourly?

No. I work on fixed price engagements so you always know the total investment upfront. No timesheets, no surprises, no creeping invoices.

How long does a typical project take?

Brand identity projects typically run four to six weeks. Websites take four to eight weeks. Combined engagements usually run eight to twelve weeks. I will give you a clear timeline in the proposal.

What if my budget is smaller than that range?

Let's still have the conversation. We can often phase the work or prioritise what matters most first so you can start strong and build from there.

Can you work with what we already have?

Absolutely. Not everyone needs to start from scratch. If your brand has good bones, I can refine and extend it rather than rebuild. That is what the initial conversation is for, figuring out the right approach.

What if we are not sure what we need?

That is exactly why the first conversation exists. Most clients do not arrive with a brief. They arrive with a problem. I will help you figure out the right path before anything is scoped or priced.

How quickly can you start and what if we need it faster?

I respond to every enquiry within twenty four hours, and most projects begin within one to two weeks. If you are on a tighter timeline, I can accommodate accelerated schedules. Rush projects are priced to reflect the compressed timeline, and I will always be upfront about what that looks like before we commit.

Do you build in Webflow?

Yes. Every website I design, I build in Webflow. You get a fast, responsive, SEO friendly site with a CMS you can actually use, without needing a separate developer.
